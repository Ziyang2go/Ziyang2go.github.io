enum VRay.ScannedMaterialParams.Plain
Enumerator
PL_NONE 

summary>No plain strategy.

summary>The material is homogenous, an averaged brdf is used instead texture (LighShot::vb), uv mapping must be provided.

PL_HMG 

summary>the material is homogenous, symmetrized brdf is used instead texture (LightShot::vbs), no uv mapping is needed.

PL_HMGSYM 

summary>the material has symmetric average brdf but small details are existing (sand,carpaint, ). the texture is used after scrambling , no uv mapping is needed.
